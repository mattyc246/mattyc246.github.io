About Me

Brief Intro

Hello there, my name is Matthew Cross!
I used to work in Automotive, but I decided to make a career change into Coding. Feel free to get to know me if you wish.

Where Am I From

I was born in a town called Scunthorpe in England. I lived there until I was 15 years old and I then moved to Malaysia. I have lived here until today.

What am I doing in Malaysia?

I studied at Alice Smith International School in Seri Kembangan for 4 years. After finishing there I returned to the UK to study Automotive Design Technology at Sheffield Hallam. After 2 years I returned to Malaysia to work in Automotive until now.

What do I like about Malaysia?
  • NASI LEMAK!
  • Everything "boleh!"
  • Malaysian Hospitality
  • The weather
  • The beaches
  • The "rakyat" of Malaysia
